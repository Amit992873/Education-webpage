Basics of computers

A computer is an electronic machine that can receive, process, store, and output data. It operates under the control of programs, which are sets of instructions written in various programming languages. Computers are used in almost every field today, including education, medicine, business, and entertainment. They can perform millions of calculations per second, making them essential for tasks that require speed and accuracy. Modern computers come in many forms, from large desktop systems to portable laptops and smartphones, all designed to make our lives easier and more efficient.
